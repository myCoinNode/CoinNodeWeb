CoinNode's Community Guidelines

Rule 1) Be Respectful
This rule is simply common sense. Just be respectful.

Rule 2) Offensive Speech
again, common sense. Offensive speech such as discrimination, racial slurs, etc is very punishable and you may be subject to removal from this server without warning practicing these acts.
Rule 3) Illegal acts
Don’t practice (or make an effort to practice) potentially illegal acts with this service, doing this will lead to removal from the server and you will be reported to the authorities.
Rule 4) Exploiting / Hacking
Exploits of any kind is disallowed and highly ban able. Refrain from trying to exploit any of our services. (It’s not cool-)
Rule 5) Golden rule
Treat this server as you would treat your friend. Friends don’t typically like to be treated in disrespect, nor do that like to be exploited and any acts along those lines. In that case, we’re your friends and we don’t like to be treated like that either. Help us create a better experience for everyone in this server, don’t be rude. Just follow the rules.
